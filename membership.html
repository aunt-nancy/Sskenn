Common Questions

Membership essentials.

Can I cancel Virtual Skin Coaching anytime?

Yes. Virtual Skin Coaching is month-to-month and you can cancel at any time before your next billing date — no fees, no questions asked.

What's the difference between the Mini Consultation and Virtual Coaching?

The Mini Consultation is a one-time $50 email review. Virtual Coaching is an ongoing $49/month relationship with monthly check-ins, routine adjustments, and direct messaging.

Are the products included in Virtual Coaching?

Products are sold separately at a 15% member discount. The Glow Program is the tier that includes a curated product bundle.

Who delivers the Glow Program?

You're paired one-to-one with a licensed consultant from our marketplace — a dermatologist, esthetician, or skincare specialist matched to your skin profile.

What if I'm not happy with my consultant match?

Just message us within the first 7 days and we'll rematch you with a different consultant at no extra cost.
