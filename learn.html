Ingredients Library

Know exactly what you're putting on your skin.

Every key ingredient in plain language — what it's best for, what it does, and what to be careful pairing it with.

Brightening · Barrier

Niacinamide

Best forOily skin, redness, pores, uneven tone
What it doesSupports the skin barrier and improves the look of tone and texture.
Pairs wellWell tolerated with nearly all ingredients.
Anti-Aging · Renewal

Retinol

Best forFine lines, texture, breakouts, uneven tone
What it doesEncourages cell turnover to smooth and refine skin over time.
Avoid withStrong acids in the same routine; always pair with daytime SPF.
Hydration

Hyaluronic Acid

Best forDryness, dehydration, all skin types
What it doesDraws water into the skin for a plumper, more comfortable feel.
TipApply to damp skin, then seal with moisturizer.
Brightening · Antioxidant

Vitamin C

Best forDark spots, dullness, environmental defense
What it doesBrightens the look of skin and supports an even, radiant tone.
TipIntroduce slowly if your skin is sensitive.
Exfoliating

Salicylic Acid

Best forAcne, clogged pores, oily skin, texture
What it doesGently clears inside the pore to reduce the look of breakouts.
Avoid withOther strong exfoliants or retinol on the same night.
Barrier · Soothing

Ceramides

Best forDry, sensitive, compromised skin
What it doesHelps replenish the barrier for lasting comfort and moisture.
Pairs wellWell tolerated with nearly all ingredients.
